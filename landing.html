The Problem

Indian banks waste
weeks on a single
credit appraisal.

  • 01

    4–6 weeks of manual work to produce a single Credit Appraisal Memo from multi-source data — GST, ITRs, bank statements, legal records.

  • 02

    Subjective bias creeps into every assessment. No two analysts produce the same scoring for the same file.

  • 03

    Red flags buried in unstructured PDFs, court records, and news sources go undetected until it's too late.

  • 04

    Circular trading fraud in GST filings inflates revenues — and no automated system catches it at scale.
